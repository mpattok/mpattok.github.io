Topic #1: Union Busting

Unions allow workers and employers to negotiate on more equal footing. However, many employers try their hardest to prevent workers from organizing into unions, thus creating an imbalance of power
Stakeholders: workers (specifically employees), business owners, politicians, union leaders/organizers

Topic #2: Corporate Media

Media sources such as news outlets and social media platforms comprise a large portion of information consumed by the public. Most news outlets and social media platforms are owned by corporations, with their main objective being profit rather than spreading accurate information.
Stakeholders: general public, news outlets, media corporations
