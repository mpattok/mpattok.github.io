Note on the Reitter translation. It combines what is usually presented as Chapters 4-6 into one chapter, and does the same for Chapters 26-32. It also combines Part 7 (The Accumulation of Capital) and Part 8 (Primitive Accumulation) into a single part, Capital's Process of Accumulation. As a result of these changes, the Reitter translation has 25 chapters in 7 parts instead of the usual 33 chapters in 8 parts, and the same numbered chapters do not contain the same content.
